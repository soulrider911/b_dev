About Me

I am very Mechanical by nature

Ever since I was a kid I loved taking things apart to see how they worked. Its this process that I feel really helps one better understand the meanings behind what you are doing, how to do it and more important, why you are doing what you are.

The concepts, messaging, & content, all rises over the most elaborate interface, design, or application. It is these “concepts & messages” that should drive the design, and harnesses and present the content on a pedistal.

Life is such a colorful place!

With color, you set a mood, direct the user, and evoke a feeling. Color is the foundation for good design, and can truly make or break an experience. With so many colors to choose from, with different interprepations to each, finding the right colors can some times be quite challenging.

I am a huge proponent of leveraging color to invite, excite, and idinicate to the user a meaning. Like well laid out type, color can get the user into a rythm and guide them through an experience, adding that estra touch to really make an experience as good as it can be.
